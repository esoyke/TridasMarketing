Questionnaires

Tridas eWriter® is a user-friendly web-based questionnaire that enables parents and teachers to rapidly complete interviews, anytime, anywhere, using any standard Internet browser. The patient's information is organized in a logical, practical format that is easy to read and understand.

Tridas eWriter

The questionnaires are an essential step in your child’s assessment and should be completed by the parents and teachers no later than 10 days prior to the first evaluation appointment. We assemble the parents’ and teachers’ responses to the online interview into a report and place it in the child’s file for the doctor’s review prior to the appointment.

 

 

There is a one-time non-refundable charge of $50.00 to process the interview data completed online.
